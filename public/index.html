MANCALA ONLINE

Welcome to Mancala Online!
Mancala is one of the oldest games known to man, and is still popular all around the world! Easy to learn but hard to master, Mancala is a fun and challenging game that anyone can play. And now, with Mancala Online, you and your friends can play together, on any device, anywhere, at any time! Click one of the links below to get started!
Using Mancala Online is simple and intuitive! Click "Start Game" to begin a new play session, and enter a nickname you'd like to play as! You will be given an invite code, as well as a link you can use to return to your play session any time. You can share the invite code with a friend, and they can enter it in the "Join Game" menu to join your game! At any time, you can leave the session and return by simply using your personal game URL. Have fun!
